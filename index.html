Why MedBlock

Physician solutions

Believe it or not, patients aren't the only group suffering from our modern healthcare. How about every doctor who aims to eradicate disease but is instead taught to manage and stabilize a patient's vitals long enough to discharge them and fill up the next bed? Not to mention, our doctors are assigned floors of patients to deal with each day and barely have even 15 minutes to spend with each patient. Physicians don't have enough TIME and all the stress in the world!

Patient solutions

Do you know what is the biggest problem in healthcare today? We don't even have access to our own medical information! We are rarely if ever told the complete truth about what's really going on in our bodies and as a result, we - the patients - are continuously marginalized and left to the whim of greedy administrators who treat healthcare like any another business.

Professional staff solutions

If we're going to mention how doctors are overwhelmed by the current system, how about a shout-out for the nurses who have to stay overnight multiple times each week while dealing with a dearth of resources at hospitals, clinics and rehab centers as well as record systems that remain disorganized, siloed and difficult to use. Nurses are not rewarded properly for the extraordinary amount of time they spend with their patients.

The MedBlock solution

Fortunately, there is something we can do about this with blockchain technology - and MedBlock is our solution!

MedBlock is a Decentralized Electronic Health Record (EHR) system on the blockchain that will reform healthcare around the world by finally placing the entire depth of a patient's medical history in the hands of their rightful owners - not hospitals, not insurance providers, not pharmaceuticals - but the patients themselves!
